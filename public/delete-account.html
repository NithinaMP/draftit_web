⚠ Account & Data Deletion

Delete Your Account

You can permanently remove your DraftIt account and all associated data at any time. Here's exactly how.

📱

Delete from the App

The fastest way — takes under a minute from inside DraftIt.

1
Open DraftIt and make sure you're signed in to the account you want to delete.
2
Tap the Settings icon in the bottom navigation bar.
3
Select Delete Account.
4
Confirm the deletion when prompted. Your account and all data will be queued for permanent removal.
🗂️

What Gets Deleted

Upon confirmation, we permanently purge the following within 30 days:

Your profile and authentication credentials
All lecture recordings and transcripts
AI-generated notes, summaries, and exam questions
Resumes and career documents created in the app
Any saved preferences and usage history
⚠️

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please download any notes or resumes you want to keep before requesting deletion.

⏱️

Timeline

In-app deletions are processed immediately upon confirmation.
Email requests are acknowledged within 2 business days.
All data is fully purged from our servers within 30 days of the request.
