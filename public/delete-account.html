Delete Your Account

Last updated: June 2026

We respect your right to control your data. You can permanently delete your DraftIt account and all associated data at any time — directly from within the app, or by contacting us if you no longer have access.

⚠️ This action is permanent. Deleting your account removes all your data from our systems. This cannot be undone. Please make sure you want to proceed before continuing.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app

This is the fastest way. Follow these steps:

1
Open DraftIt
Launch the app on your Android device and sign in if you haven't already.
2
Go to Settings
Tap the Settings tab in the bottom navigation bar (bottom right icon).
3
Scroll to Account section
Scroll down until you see the Account section with Sign Out and Delete Account options.
4
Tap "Delete Account"
Tap the red Delete Account button. A confirmation sheet will appear.
5
Confirm your identity
For email accounts: enter your current password to confirm.
For Google accounts: you will be prompted to re-authenticate with Google.
6
Tap "Delete" — done
Your account and all data are permanently deleted immediately. You will be signed out automatically.
✓ Instant deletion. When you delete from inside the app, your account is removed from Firebase Authentication and all your cloud data is permanently deleted immediately — no waiting period.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you have already uninstalled the app or cannot sign in, you can request account deletion by emailing us directly.

Send your request to:

Email us at the support address listed on our homepage with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered email address in the message body.

We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days of receiving your request and send you a confirmation email once complete.

What data gets deleted

When your account is deleted — whether from the app or via email request — the following data is permanently removed:

Data Type Storage Location Deleted When
Account credentials
Email, name, sign-in method		 Firebase Authentication Immediately
Lecture notes
Transcripts, summaries, key points		 Firebase Firestore Immediately
Master Profile
Skills, experience, education, projects, certifications		 Firebase Firestore Immediately
Job Applications
Generated resumes, match scores, JD analysis		 Firebase Firestore Immediately
Exam questions
Generated questions and evaluations		 Device local storage (Hive) On app uninstall
Audio recordings
Lecture audio files		 Device local storage only — never uploaded permanently On app uninstall
Note on local data: Data stored on your device (exam questions, syllabus progress, cached notes) is automatically removed when you uninstall the DraftIt app from your phone. It is not accessible by us and does not need to be separately deleted.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process or what happens to your data, visit our Privacy Policy or reach out via the contact on our homepage.
